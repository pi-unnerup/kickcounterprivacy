🍼 Baby Kick Counter

Count your baby’s kicks — Support

Baby Kick Counter is a simple, friendly way to track your baby’s movements during pregnancy. Tap to log a kick, see your daily count, and review activity over time.

Getting started

  • Log a kick — tap the big baby-foot button each time you feel a kick.
  • Daily count — the number shows your current counting session; it resets after 5 minutes without a kick, so each session starts fresh.
  • Undo — made a mistake? Use the undo button to remove the last kick.
  • History — see the times of today’s kicks.
  • Graph — view the last 7 days of activity in 15-minute bars; scroll back through the week.
  • Theme — switch between a pink or blue colour theme from the palette button.
  • Apple Watch — log kicks from your wrist; they sync to your iPhone.

Frequently asked questions

Why did my count go back to zero?

The on-screen number tracks your current session. After 5 minutes with no new kick, the session ends and the counter resets — your kicks are still saved and appear in History and the Graph.

Does it work with my Apple Watch?

Yes. The watch companion lets you log kicks from your wrist, and they sync with the iPhone app so your totals, history, and graph stay up to date.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your kicks are stored only on your devices and are never sent to us or any third party. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

Remove individual kicks in the app, or delete the app from your device to erase all stored data.

Is this medical advice?

No. Baby Kick Counter is a tracking tool, not a medical device. If you’re ever worried about your baby’s movements, contact your midwife or doctor right away.
