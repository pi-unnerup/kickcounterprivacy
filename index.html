🍼 Baby Kick Counter

Privacy Policy · Last updated 19 June 2026

Baby Kick Counter (“the app”) helps expectant parents count their baby’s kicks. Your privacy matters, and the app is built to keep your data on your own devices. This policy explains what the app stores and what it does not do.

In short: the app does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. Everything you log stays on your device (and your paired Apple Watch). There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Information the app stores

  • Kick records — the date and time of each kick you log.
  • Preferences — small settings such as your chosen colour theme.

This information is stored locally on your device (in the app’s private storage). It is not uploaded to the developer or to any server.

Apple Watch sync

If you use the Apple Watch companion, your kick records are synced directly between your iPhone and your paired Apple Watch using Apple’s WatchConnectivity. This transfer happens between your own devices — the data is not sent to the developer or any third party.

What the app does not collect

  • No name, email, account, or login.
  • No location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for tracking.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect data.

App Store reviews

The app may occasionally ask you to rate it using Apple’s standard review prompt. This is handled entirely by Apple; the developer does not receive any personal information from it.

Your data & deletion

You are in control of your data. You can remove individual entries within the app, and deleting the app from your device permanently removes all stored kick records and preferences.

Children

The app is intended for expectant parents and is not directed at children.

Not medical advice. Baby Kick Counter is a simple tracking tool, not a medical device, and does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. If you have any concern about your baby’s movements, contact your midwife or doctor.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.
